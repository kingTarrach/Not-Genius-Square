Not Genius Square Instructions:
- Click on a piece to make it enter move mode.
- Use WASD to move the piece in cardinal directions.
- Use R to rotate the piece.
- Press enter to set the piece down, you will not be able to set a piece down if it collides
with another piece or blocking circle.
- The game is won when all board tiles are occupied with no overlap.
- Restart to create a new board.
